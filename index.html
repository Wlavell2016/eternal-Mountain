About Our Studio

“There is no value in anything until it is finished.” ― Chingis Khan

Ahjibeck Tech

A First Nations owned studio; working towards all nations rising up togethor.

What we do?

The best description is digital data story telling. We take your immense volumes of data about your organization and turn that into a story about what your organization does. If the output is a web site, info-graphic,map or dynamic charts it makes no difference. Your story is our goal.

Where we work

We work both remotely and onsite and have done rapid response missions to: Lebanon, Tanzania, Myanmar and Thailand. If you need IM,GIS or Web Development support we can the tools and expertise to get your project rolling.

A a creative indigenous run studio specializing in bringing your data alive through an array of methods including: Web Development, GIS, and Info-graphics. We also support the creation and deployment of data collection tools as well. We work with First Nations the United Nations and small businesses. If you have data that needs processing or a creative project that needs creating; we can meet your needs.

When you have a mountain of tasks that you dont feel like doing; think of Ahjibeck Tech.


Our services

Our Services

We specialize in the collection, creation and visualization of spatial data using webbased and desktop methods to meet our clients needs.

  • Web Development

    We can meet your web development needs with our full stack web development services. We specailize in Ruby on Rails and front end Javascript frameworks. With our skils we can help you get your idea, onto the web rapidly.

  • GIS Geomatics

    We are a full spectrum GIS service provider and can meet your spatial data needs through the entire range of tasks. From field data collection, to data processing to cartographic plotting to spatial app development.

  • Information Management

    Being able to target and coordinate a rapid response in a humanitarian crisis requires strategic desision making based on data and analysis. With years of experience in GIS, planning and data visualization we are able to respond to multi-sectoral information needs and help those in need rapidly.
