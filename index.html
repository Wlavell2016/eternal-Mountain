About Our Studio

“There are three responses to a piece of design: yes, no and wow!”― Milton Glaser

What we do?

The best description is digital data story telling. We take the immense volumes of data about your organization and turn that into a story about what your organization does. If the output is a web site, info-graphic,map or dynamic charts; it makes no difference. Your story is our goal.

Who we work for?

We primarily work with First Nations, Urban Planning Specialist and Humanitarian Aid Agencies. Making informed fact based decisions is a requirement in all of those fields and we provide the tools and data to support this. We create data stories to help you make better decisions.

Where we work

We work both remotely and onsite and have done rapid response missions to: Lebanon, Tanzania, Myanmar and Thailand. If you need IM,GIS or Web Development support we can the tools and expertise to get your project rolling.

Our business

A creative indigenous run studio specializing in giving life and meaning to your data, through an array of methods including: Web Development, GIS, and Info-graphics.

We also support the creation and deployment of data collection tools as well. So many organizations do amazing work and have the data and numbers to prove it. Let us bring your data to life and show others what great work you do. Our business is singing your data story to the mountains.

Our Services

We specialize in the collection, creation and visualization of data using webbased and desktop methods to meet our clients needs.

  • Web Development

    We work with a modern frameworks and tools to turn your data into dynamic visualizations. With our tools we can help you get your story, onto the web rapidly.

  • GIS Geomatics

    We are a full spectrum GIS service provider from field data collection, to data processing to cartographic plotting to spatial app development.

  • Info-Graphics
